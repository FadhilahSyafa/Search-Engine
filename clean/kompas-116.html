https://go.kompas.com/read/2020/10/15/170600574/singapore-and-hong-kong-travel-bubble-to-launch-soon singapore and hong kong travel bubble to launch soon a singapore and hong kong travel bubble will be set up between the two cities to reestablish overseas travel links the travel bubble will lift the hurdle of quarantine for visiting foreigners from singapore and hong kong hong kongs commerce secretary edward yau and singapores transport mister ong ye kung said travelers under the scheme would need to get negative covid19 test results and travel on dedicated flights further details on the singapore and hong kong travel bubble plans including the launch date will be fleshed out in the coming weeks they said it is a safe careful but significant step forward to revive air travel and provide a model for future collaboration with other parts of the world singapores ong said for hong kong which has banned nonresidents since march the special travel corridor with singapore is its first resumption of travel ties with another city travelers from mainland china and neighboring macau still face 14 days in quarantine singapore has already announced pacts on essential business and official travel from china indonesia japan malaysia south korea and opened unilaterally to general visitors from brunei new zealand vietnam and most of australia
